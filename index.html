Arithmetic

is the most basic math. In arithmetic you will learn the 4 basic functions + - / x and then later more complex functions such as the square root.

Geometry

is a branch of math that deals with things like lines, angles, shapes and points.

Algebra

is one of the most important parts of math. It is where you learn that you can use letters in math, and you
have to solve for these letters.

Trigonometry

is basically a higher level geometry class. This is where you learn new functions like sine, cosine and tangent. Trigonometry is very useful later on.

Calculus

is another very important part of math. There is two types of calculus, differential and integral calculus. In differential calculus, you learn how to find the slope of a non-linear function. In integral calculus, you learn
how to find the area under the slope of a non-linear function.
